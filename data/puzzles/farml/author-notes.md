We got the idea for this puzzle by thinking
about how we could use Gradescope as the basis
of a puzzle mechanic.
One person had the idea we ought to do a programming assignment;
another person had the idea that Gradescope naturally
gives a bunch of fractions, making continued fractions
a viable mechanism.
These two ideas both make an appearance in the puzzle.

The idea to create a super relay for the programming contest
came about because we were thinking about USACO,
which famously has cow mascots.
At the same time, [FARML] is a real contest,
so we got super excited when it clicked that we could
make an ARML event for the cows.
