Originally, we were going to have "banned book" instead of a "band book", but
then we realized that we had the opportunity to write a K-pop puzzle and
couldn't resist the temptation.
Serena mentions that the inspiration for the Korean mechanic
was all the nonsense strings that she would accidentally type
whenever she forgot to change the keyboard layout into Korean.

The idea of using numbers in K-pop group names is not new;
see [Site Visits](https://reddothunt.sg/2020/puzzle/site-visits),
from RedDot 2020 hunt.
