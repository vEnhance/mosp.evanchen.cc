## Athletics

This event was a [relay event styled after ARML](/static/2022/olympics/A.pdf).
During the actual event, teams needed to run back and forth to pass answers too.

## Cross-country skiing

This event was a [cryptic crossword](/static/2022/olympics/C.html)
where two of the entries have had a three-letter substring deleted.

## Golf

This was a code golf event, where teams needed to
write a function to check whether a sudoku grid was valid.
Upon completion, they received a [letter sudoku][sudoku].

[sudoku]: https://tinyurl.com/y5s9qs9l

## Nordic combined

This was a game where teams were shown
[slides with two interleaved country names](/static/2022/olympics/N.pdf)
and needed to unweave them.
Upon completion, they received three more strings:

```text
LKUYXRGYEMZSBTOMAUNRG
EVANSDWATUINATUI
LMAIUTHRITUHANIIAUS
```
