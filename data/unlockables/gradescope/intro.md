You head to the northeast part of the campus and find,
to your mild disbelief, a herd of cows hanging around outside the grader's headquarters.
Not just one herd, in fact.
No, more like a dozen herds of maybe a dozen-odd cows each.

But oddly enough, the cows aren't standing up like you'd expect.
They're all... _sitting_. With laptops. Typing on the laptops.

You promptly give up trying to make sense of the situation.

From a few paces away, a farmer comes running towards you.
You can see a name tag that says "Farmer John" on it.
"Oh, the assistant director! Thank god!"

"Uh... can I help you?"

"Heck yes you can!"
Farmer John practically screams at you in frustration.
"I can't herd my cattle! They just sit around all day coding!"

Cows coding. Sounds familiar enough...

_What should Farmer John say to his cows?_
