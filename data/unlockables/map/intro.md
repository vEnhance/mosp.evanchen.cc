Having done your fair share of problem-solving at MOSP,
it is time to say goodbye.
You've figured out where the exit is:
the analog of the place you entered.

_It's just like the first time_, you think to yourself.
Once again, a secret tunnel behind the map.

You stare at the tunnel, with a bit of fear inside you.
After all, it goes further down into darkness.
_Is this really the right route? It sounds crazy._

You try to use your primary colors,
but this time, nothing happens.
It's nighttime now on this side of the world,
and without the sunlight, your chroma key doesn't function.

The moon still glows as brightly
as it did when you came here, so you can still see around you.
But Steve is nowhere to be found.
All you see is the pile of stones from when you first met Steve.
The moonlight is reflecting off the stones,
causing them to grow almost unnaturally bright,
and the glare grabs your attention.

Now that you think about it, you wonder what those stones are for.
Did they belong to Steve?
