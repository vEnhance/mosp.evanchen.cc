To the southwest, you find yourself surrounded by a set of enormous towers.
The towers are so tall that you can barely see the tops of them.

You find Steve standing near the base of the furthest tower.

"Oh hey, ya found the towers," Steve grins at you.

"What are they?" you ask.

"Zoom communication. Heard of it?" Steve says.
"This fancy tech's all black magic to me.
Wireless signals, ya know? The bits are *in the air*.  Crazy stuff."

"And yet..." Steve looks upwards towards one of the towers as his voice trails off.
"We've been having problems with our Zoom calls lately.
I tried figuring out what's wrong, but man, I just don't get this stuff at all."

"So, your job!" Steve grins again as he hands you yet another map.
"Good luck!"

And with that Steve disappears into thin air again.
